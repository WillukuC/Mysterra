>[!info]
>Necromancy cantrip
>**Casting Time**: 1 action
>**Range**: Touch
>**Components**: V, S
>**Duration**: Instant

You touch a living creature that has 0 hit points. The creature becomes stable. This spell has no effect on [[undead]] or [[Construct|constructs]].<br>
>[!info] Available to:
>Artificer, Cleric
