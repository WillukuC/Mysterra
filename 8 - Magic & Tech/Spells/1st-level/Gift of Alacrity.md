>[!info]
>1st-level divination
>**Casting Time**: 1 action
>**Range**: Touch
>**Components**: V, S
>**Duration**: 8 hours

You touch a willing creature. For the duration, the target can add 1d8 to its initiative rolls.<br>
>[!info] Available to:
>Artificer, Bard, Cleric, Paladin
